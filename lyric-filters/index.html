Lyric Filter Definitnions for Business Music

Green Lyric Filter

We use songs that contain no profanity. There will be no implicit or explicit references to sexual activity or innuendo, alcohol use, drug references, or violence.

Yellow Lyric Filter

We use radio edits of songs that eliminate, cover, or partially eliminate the most offensive profanities. Be advised that some songs may contain the words “hell” and “damn,” words that are considered radio friendly. Also, songs in this category may contain explicit references to drinking and may have implicit references to sexual activity or innuendo, religious references, drug references, and violence

Orange Lyric Filter

We adhere to the same standards that a United States radio broadcast facility is required to maintain 6 a.m. to 10 p.m. under FCC law. We use radio edits of songs that eliminate, cover, or partially eliminate the most offensive profanities. Be advised that some songs may contain the words “hell”, “damn”, “bitch” or “ass,” words that are considered radio friendly. Also, there may be explicit lyrics that depict or describe sexual activity or innuendo, alcohol use, drug references, religious references, and violence.
